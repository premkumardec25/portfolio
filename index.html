About Me

Technical Writer and Instructional Designer specializing in software documentation, eLearning development, and AI-assisted content creation, with proven experience delivering user-centric content across Supply Chain, Healthcare, Education, and e-Governance domains.

I bring 5 plus years of experience in Instructional Design, creating engaging eLearning courses and instructor-led training programs using the ADDIE and SAM models. My work includes instructional storyboarding, knowledge checks, assessments, and certification exams, leveraging modern tools and AI-assisted content development techniques to enhance learner engagement and improve learning outcomes.

In addition, I have 7 years of experience in Technical Writing. I have developed and maintained user manuals, training manuals, help files, installation guides, release notes, RFPs, and RFIs while collaborating closely with product development and quality assurance teams in Agile and Waterfall environments.

Known for strong stakeholder collaboration, attention to detail, and the ability to simplify complex concepts, I am passionate about delivering clear, user-centric documentation and impactful learning experiences that enable customer success and business growth.
